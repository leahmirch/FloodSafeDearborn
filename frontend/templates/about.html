About FloodSafeDearborn

FloodSafeDearborn is a community-focused platform aimed at providing real-time flood information and resources to the residents of Dearborn, Michigan. Due to low elevations, aging sewer systems, and the proximity of the Rouge River, Dearborn is particularly susceptible to flooding during heavy rainfall. Our mission is to empower residents to stay informed, prepared, and protected from the dangers of flooding.

Project Description

FloodSafeDearborn combines innovative technology with community participation to create a reliable and user-friendly platform for flood awareness. Through real-time data, historical flood trends, and customizable notifications, our platform ensures that residents can make informed decisions to safeguard their homes, families, and businesses during flood events.

By offering interactive tools and valuable resources, FloodSafeDearborn addresses the urgent need for flood preparedness in Dearborn, helping to mitigate risks and enhance community resilience.

Key Features

  • Interactive Flood Map: A live, real-time map displaying flooding hotspots reported by community members, allowing users to stay updated on current conditions.
  • Customizable Notifications: Users can input their address and receive alerts for flooding incidents reported within a chosen radius.
  • Historical Flood Data: Access past flooding records to understand trends and identify high-risk areas in the city.
  • Emergency Resources: Find contact details for local authorities, tips for flood preparedness, and access to emergency services.
  • Community Reporting: Residents can report flooding incidents with descriptions, locations, and multimedia uploads, fostering a collaborative approach to flood awareness.

Why FloodSafeDearborn?

The project was inspired by the challenges faced by the Dearborn community during flood events. With infrastructure limitations and unpredictable weather patterns, residents often struggle to prepare for and respond to flooding. FloodSafeDearborn bridges this gap by offering a centralized platform for real-time information, data-driven insights, and accessible resources.

Unlike other flood preparedness solutions, FloodSafeDearborn is tailored specifically to the unique needs of the Dearborn community. Its localized focus ensures that residents have access to the most relevant and actionable information.

Our Vision

We envision a safer and more prepared Dearborn community where residents can respond proactively to flood risks. By integrating advanced technology with community-driven efforts, FloodSafeDearborn aims to set a standard for flood management and awareness in urban areas.
