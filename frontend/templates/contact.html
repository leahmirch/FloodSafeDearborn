Contact Us

We appreciate your interest in FloodSafeDearborn. Below, you'll find information on how to reach the City of Dearborn for flood-related inquiries as well as contact details for the development team behind this platform.

City of Dearborn Contact Information

  • Dearborn City Hall:

    For general flood-related concerns, infrastructure issues, or city assistance, please reach out to Dearborn City Hall.

    Phone: (313) 943-2000

    Address: 16901 Michigan Ave, Dearborn, MI 48126

    Visit City of Dearborn Website
  • Wayne County Drain Commissioner:

    If you have specific inquiries related to drainage or flood prevention projects, contact the Wayne County Drain Commissioner's Office.

    Phone: (313) 224-3620

    Visit Wayne County Drain Commissioner's Website

Contact the Development Team

If you have feedback or suggestions regarding FloodSafeDearborn, please feel free to reach out to our development team using the details below.

  • GitHub Repository: https://github.com/leahmirch/FloodSafeDearborn

Additional Assistance

For emergency situations, please contact local emergency services by dialing 911. For non-emergency flood-related issues, you can reach out to the City of Dearborn or the Wayne County Drain Commissioner using the details above.
